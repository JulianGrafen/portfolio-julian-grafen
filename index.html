PROJECTS

  • SOCIALVIEW

    Read More

    SocialView is a project initated at Code & Context. In a group we identified the problem that usual social media marketing is quite expensive for small restaurants. So we thought about how we can offer cheaper and effortless social media marketing for restaurants. As result we developed a SASS solution. In the restaurant a TV displays the social media posts made from the customers. As soon as a customer posts a picture from his meal under a specific hashtag, the picture is shown with the username on the TV in the restaurant. This animates the customers to take photos and publish them. This results in social media presence for the restaurant and an unique customer experience for the customer. We have used a TypeScript and NestJS based backend and a React frontend. We worked with the Meta and Google API for displaying customer posts.
